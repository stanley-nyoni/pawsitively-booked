Frequently Asked Questions

To book a pet sitter, you can either sign up for an account or log in if you already have one. Once you are logged in, you can search for pet sitters in your area and view their profiles. You can then send a booking request to the pet sitter of your choice.

You can pay for pet sitting services using a credit card or PayPal. Payments are processed securely through our website, and you will receive a confirmation email once the payment has been processed.

If you need to cancel a booking, you can do so through your account dashboard. Please note that there may be cancellation fees depending on the pet sitter's cancellation policy.

You can contact customer support by emailing pawsitivelybookings@gmail.com or by calling our customer support number at 0611440669. Our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have.

You can update your profile information by logging into your account and clicking on the "Account" tab and selecting "My Profile". From there, you can edit your profile information, including your name, email address, and phone number.

Creating a profile allows us to verify your identity and ensure that you are a legitimate user. It also allows pet sitters to learn more about you and your pet before accepting a booking request.

You can leave a review for a pet sitter by logging into your account and clicking on the "Bookings" tab. From there, you can select the booking you would like to review and leave a rating and comment for the pet sitter.

Creating a profile for your pet allows pet sitters to learn more about your pet's needs and preferences before accepting a booking request. It also helps us ensure that your pet is up-to-date on vaccinations and other health requirements.

You can update your pet's profile information by logging into your account and clicking on the "My Dog Profile" tab. From there, you can edit your pet's profile information, including their name, breed, age, and any special needs or preferences.

You can register as many dogs as you like on your account. Each dog will have their own profile, and you can book pet sitting services for each dog separately.

To delete your account, please contact customer support at pawsitivelybookings@gmail.com or call our customer support number at 0611440669. Our customer support team will assist you with deleting your account. Please note that once your account is deleted, all of your information will be permanently removed from our system.

To manage bookings for your facility, you can log into your account and click on the "Facility" tab. From there, you can view and manage all of your bookings, including accepting or declining booking requests, updating availability, and setting pricing and policies.

You can update your facility's profile information by logging into your account and clicking on the "Facility" tab. From there, you can edit your facility's profile information, including your facility's name, address, contact information, and any special policies or requirements.

When registering your facility, you will need to provide basic information about your facility, including your facility's name, address, contact information, and any special policies or requirements. You will also need to provide payment information for processing bookings and payments.
