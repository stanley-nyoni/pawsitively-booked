FAQs

To sign up for an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the page. You will be prompted to enter your email address and create a password. Once you have completed the sign-up process, you will be able to create a profile for your pet or facility.

To book a pet care provider, search for providers in your area and filter by services offered. Once you have found a provider that meets your needs, click on the "Book Now" button to request a booking. The provider will review your request and either accept or decline it.

To become a pet care provider, sign up for an account and create a profile for your facility. Once your profile is complete, you will be able to receive booking requests from pet owners.

To leave a review, log in to your account and navigate to the provider's profile page. Click on the "Leave a Review" button and follow the prompts to leave a review and rating.
